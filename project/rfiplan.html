Revival Fellowship International - Site Plan

Site Name

The name of the Site is Revival Fellowship International, highlighting committment to discipleship, church planting, and community outreach.

Site Description

The website will feature sections on:

  • Upcoming events
  • Sermons
  • Ministries
  • Ways to get involved

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to enhance the online presence of Revival Fellowship International, making it easier for people to connect, engage, and participate in church activities. The website will serve as a tool for:

  • Evangelism
  • Discipleship
  • Church growth

It will reach both local and international audiences, expanding the church's reach and making it easier for members to stay informed and involved.

Audience

The target audience for this website includes:

  • Church members looking to stay informed on upcoming events and sermons.
  • Potential new members interested in joining the church.
  • Local and international visitors seeking spiritual growth and resources.
  • People looking for community outreach and volunteer opportunities.

The content will be tailored to engage both the local congregation and global visitors who are interested in Revival Fellowship's ministries.

Branding

Website Logo

The logo will feature simple Christian symbols like a cross or a dove, incorporated with modern and approachable typography that appeals to a broad audience.

Color Palette

The color palette will reflect vibrant tones that create an inviting and energetic atmosphere:

  • Primary Color: – Used for call-to-action buttons, icons, and highlights to evoke warmth and positivity.
  • Secondary Color: – Used for headers, buttons, and navigation links to grab attention and create enthusiasm.
  • Accent Color: – Used for highlighting important messages, warnings, or urgent calls-to-action.
  • Background Color: – Used for the website's background to create a calming and welcoming feel, enhancing readability.

Typography

Primary Font

Font Family: Montserrat (Sans-serif)

This font will be used for headings, subheadings, and emphasis throughout the site. It provides a modern and readable style that is easy on the eyes for church-goers of all ages.

Secondary Font

Font Family: Roboto (Sans-serif)

This font will be used for body text, paragraphs, and other general content. It complements Montserrat and offers readability across different devices.

Wireframe

The homepage wireframe will be designed to show the essential sections for the church’s audience, including:

  • Hero section with a welcome message and call-to-action button
  • Upcoming events and sermon highlights
  • Ministries and volunteer opportunities
  • Navigation bar for easy access to other sections like sermons and contact

There will be two wireframe versions: one for mobile and another for desktop. The mobile version will have a collapsed navigation menu and a streamlined layout for ease of use on smaller screens.
